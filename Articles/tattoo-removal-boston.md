## New England Climate and Treatment Scheduling

### Seasonal UV Profile

Boston's northern latitude (42.3°N) and genuine four-season climate produce a favorable UV profile:

- **Summer (June-August):** UV index 7-9
- **Fall (September-November):** UV index 3-5
- **Winter (December-February):** UV index 1-3
- **Spring (March-May):** UV index 4-7

The November-through-March window delivers 5 months of UV index below 4 — an extended low-risk treatment window. Boston's cold winters encourage layered clothing that naturally protects treatment sites.

**Optimal treatment timing:** Begin September-October. Complete 3-5 sessions during the low-UV fall and winter months. Spring sessions (April-May) remain manageable with standard sun protection.

### Cold Weather Aftercare

New England winters create specific aftercare considerations. Dry indoor heat (common in Boston's older buildings) dehydrates healing skin. Windburn on exposed treatment areas compounds irritation.

**Winter aftercare adjustments:**
- Moisturize treated areas aggressively — heated indoor air drops humidity below 25%
- Protect exposed treatment sites (hands, neck, face) from wind and cold during the initial healing window
- Avoid hot showers directly on treated areas — the temperature shock stresses healing tissue

## Massachusetts Regulatory Framework

Massachusetts regulates laser procedures through the **Board of Registration in Medicine** and the **Department of Public Health Radiation Control Program**.

- Laser tattoo removal requires physician oversight
- The state's radiation safety requirements apply to medical laser facilities
- Non-physician operators require documented training and physician-delegated authority
- Massachusetts maintains relatively rigorous oversight compared to many states

**Verification:** The **Massachusetts Board of Registration in Medicine** provides online license verification. Check your provider's active licensure, board certification, and disciplinary history. Massachusetts's medical board is among the most active in the country for enforcement actions — clean records here carry real weight.
