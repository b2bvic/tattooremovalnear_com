Manhattan practices routinely offer **Pro-Nox** nitrous oxide ($150-$250 additional) and injectable lidocaine ($200-$350 extra) for comprehensive pain control. Outer borough clinics more commonly use topical **lidocaine 5%** and **Zimmer Cryo** cooling as standard.
