## NYC Climate and Healing

NYC's urban heat island effect creates 5-10°F warmer temperatures than surrounding areas. Summer heat and humidity (80-90°F, 70-80% humidity) increase infection risk through perspiration. Schedule treatments during spring (April-June) or fall (September-November).

**Sun exposure from commuting:** Subway platforms, street walking, and building-to-building travel create unavoidable UV exposure. Treated areas require physical coverage (long sleeves, pants) or mineral sunscreen reapplication every 90 minutes.

Winter's cold and dry air (25-40°F, 50-65% humidity) demands humidifiers and 3x daily moisturizing. Indoor heating in apartments and offices dehydrates skin, hardening collagen matrices.
